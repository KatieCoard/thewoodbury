Why James Bay?

Tranquility

James Bay is a peaceful residential neighbourhood where you can experience community living. You can relax in the flower laden garden or take a short walk to the ocean front.

Convenience

James Bay is conveniently located close to Cook street village and downtown Victoria. Both are within walking and cycling distance. There is also a large supermarket within walking distance as well.

Nature

James Bay is close to the ocean front where you want to take a walk along Dallas road or simply relax at the beach. Within walking distance is also Beacon Hill park, where you can go explore Beacon Hill park and visit the petting Zoo.

Our Suites

The Woodbury offers 4 fully furnished apartments: 1 studio and 3 one bedroom.

Studio

Located on the top floor, brightly lit by large windows and a central skylight. The queen bed is easy to raise/lower and hides in a closet during the day. There is a living room with a dining area, a fully equiped kitchen and 4 piece bathroom. The top floor apartment also gives you a panoramic view of James Bay.

One Bedroom

The Woodbury offers 2 one bedroom apartments. Each apartment comes with a spacious bedroom, 4 piece bath and a large bright fully equiped kitchen with a dining area. You can sit out on the patio or in the back garden and enjoy the tranquility of James Bay living.
