The weather in Victoria British Columbia is usually mild; the summer is comfortable as Victoria is on the southern end of Vancouver Island, surrounded by beautiful temperate rain forest and the waters of the Juan de Fuca and Georgia Straights. The Pacific Ocean keeps the air cool in the summer but comfortable in the winter.
If you are looking for an excellent, comfortable and convenient residence call The Woodbury Apartments. A 4 apartment, centrally located building in a quite neighbourhood within walking distance of shopping and downtown sites, The Woodbury Apartments will provide the accommodation you require.
