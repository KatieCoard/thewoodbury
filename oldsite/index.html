at the shops

at most tourist attractions; prepare for all those visitors!

For cycling enthusiasts, walkers or gardeners, the location of your furnished rental accommodation  at The Woodbury is excellent:

Bicycle storage space is available

Dallas Road, a leisurely cycle along the Victoria waterfront is at your 'doorstep'

A 5-minute cycle puts you on The Galloping Goose Trail leading to either The West Shore or Sooke or north to The Saanich Peninsula and the BC Ferry Terminal at Swartz Bay. Both trails are part of The Trans Canada Trail

A 5-minute walk to the south of The Woodbury Apartments brings you to The Dallas Road Walkway with panoramic views of The Straits of Juan de Fuca and The Olympic Mountains. Walk the Victoria waterfront and view seals, sea lions, whales, herons, otters, eagles and many sea birds. This is a favourite for the 'locals'.

For gardeners, or those appreciating beautiful gardens:

Beacon Hill Park - 200 acres of beautifully landscaped and manicured grounds complete with bridges, lakes and ponds, an alpine rock garden, and a spectacular rose garden, is at your doorstep

Abkahzi Gardens - an exquisite heritage home and garden (with a truly romantic story) is a 10-minute drive

Playfair Park (a secret hidden garden in Saanich, famous for Rhododendrons) can be reached in 10 minutes

Hatley Park Gardens on the grounds of Royal Roads University is a 20-minute drive from The Woodbury Vacation Rental Apartments

Finnerty Gardens, the 'unknown gardens' of The University of Victoria, is a 15 minute drive

The Government House, home of the Governor General, with spectacular gardens, is about 8 minutes away

Many people live in James Bay without their own transportation. The Woodbury Apartments are convenient to the bus transportation system of Greater Victoria. The bus stop for downtown is 2 blocks away; the bus stop for your return trip is 1 block away. Once in the downtown core, links to all locations of Greater Victoria can be made.

You will find a myriad of things to do to fill your spare time; all very convenient from this ideal location
