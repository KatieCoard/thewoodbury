Studio Apartment:
The bachelor studio apartment is located on the top (second) floor, and brightly lit by large windows and central skylight. The queen Murphy bed is easy to raise and lower, and 'hides' in a closet during the day. Enjoy a large living area with furnished dining corner. The equipped kitchen and 4 piece bathroom complete the unit. Each top floor apartment has a panoramic view of James Bay and the Sooke Hills from the front balcony.
