1 Bedroom Apartment:
Step up and into the 1 bedroom apartment on the main floor. The decor of the spacious living room has an oriental touch; comfortable furnishings include a single hide-a-bed for the overnight visitor, or visiting holiday family. An easy to use gas fireplace, will warm any chilly evening.
 
Each apartment comes with a spacious bedroom, 4 piece bath and large bright kitchen with an eating area. Sit outside on the patio or in the back garden, or watch the local Beacon Hill Park peacocks travelling around the neighbourhood.
